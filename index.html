About Michelle

Strengthening the bond between
humans and animals

My mission is to strengthen the bond between humans and animals by bringing insight, understanding and compassionate communication into the home. When both humans and animals feel heard and understood, harmony flows naturally – and a harmonious home is truly a happy home!

For over 20 years, I have been guiding clients through powerful healing journeys using modalities such as Crystal Dreaming, Reiki, sound healing, meditation and massage. I work with people across Australia and around the world.

In our sessions, we gently explore and clear trauma held in this lifetime and beyond. I specialise in dissolving energetic contracts from both this life and past lives, including karmic agreements that may be influencing your current experiences. This deep work creates space for freedom, clarity and transformation.
