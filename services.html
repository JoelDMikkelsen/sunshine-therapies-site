Animal Communication

Animal communication is about creating a deeper connection and understanding with your pet — gaining genuine insight into what's happening in their world. Most of us already communicate with our pets through behaviour and body language. Animal communication takes it a meaningful step further.

Through intuitive energy connection, Michelle can exchange feelings, sensations, thoughts, and images with your animal, providing you with a real window into their experience and perspective.

This can help with…
  • Changed behaviour
  • Moving home
  • Additions to the family
  • Changes in family dynamics
  • Animal health concerns
  • Boarding anxiety & reassurance
  • Separation anxiety
  • Stress in the household
  • Vet assistance & support
  • Communicating with deceased pets

With animal communication, I lovingly support pets who are adjusting to boarding. I communicate reassurance, calm their anxieties, and help them understand that their family is temporarily away and will be coming back for them.
